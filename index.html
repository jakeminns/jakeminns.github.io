3rd Year Research Project

Electron Monopole interactions Along a Two Dimensional Plane

The project aim was to understand the trajectories of electrons confined to a two dimensional electron gas (2DEG) and situated above a magnetic monopole. This was accomplished by constructing a computer model was using the programming language Fortran 95.

4th Year Research Project

Investigation Into The Structure of Methylammonium Lead Iodide

Photovoltaic devices based on methylammonium lead iodide have seen a dramatic rise in efficiency despite an incomplete understanding of its microscopic conduction mechanism. Single crystal and powdered samples of methylammonium lead iodide were synthesised and analysed through the application of the maximum entropy method on Neutron and X-ray diffraction data.
