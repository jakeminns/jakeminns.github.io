3rd Year Reaserch Project

Electron Monopole interactions Along a Two Dimensional Plane

The project aim was to understand the trajectories of electrons confined to a two dimensional electron gas (2DEG) when situated above a magnetic monopole. This was acomplished by constructing a computer model was using the programming language Fortran 95.

4th Year Reaserch Project

Investigation Into The Structure of Methylammonium Lead Iodide

Photovoltaic devices based on methylammonium lead iodide have seen a dramatic rise in efficiency despite an incomplete understanding of its microscopic conduction mechanism. Single crystal and powdered samples of methylammonium lead iodide were synthesised. The structure was investigated through the analysis of Neutron and X-ray diffraction data using the maximum entropy method.
